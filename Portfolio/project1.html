Project: Matchmaking

Matchmaking is an application that was created in the course of Product Development in Media Technology using the "Design­-build­-test" method. The application was made in collaboration with Sigma Industry in Umeå. Sigma is a consulting company that has lots of assignments and consultants. They needed help finding an effective way to match these components. In the future, the application will help Sigma know what they need to take in for compotes. But this application focuses only on matchmaking between consultant and mission.
